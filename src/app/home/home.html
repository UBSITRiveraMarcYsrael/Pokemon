The World of Pokémon Regions

Kanto Region History

The Kanto region, named after the real-world Kantō region of Japan, introduced the original generation of Pokémon journeys. It is historically known for its industrialized cities like Saffron and Vermilion, contrasting with rural settings like Pallet Town. Kanto centers heavily around scientific advancements, famously housing the Pokémon Lab on Cinnabar Island and the corporate Silph Co. headquarters.

Johto Region History

Located directly west of Kanto, the Johto region leans deep into ancient mythologies, cultural heritage, and legendary traditions. Cities like Ecruteak and Violet feature historical architecture, traditional pagodas, and shrines dedicated to legendary birds and guardians. Johto is famous for its natural landmarks, Apricorn trees, and a deep spiritual connection shared between humans and nature.
